# Official Liturgical Reference Sources (Autorisierte Quellen)

## Overview

All liturgical texts, hymns, sacrament structures, and readings used in LiturgyBridge must be sourced directly from authorized ecclesiastical sources and official digital libraries.

---

## Authoritative Digital Libraries & Primary Sources

### 1. Church Slavonic & Russian (Церковнославянский и Русский)
- **Azbyka.ru Liturgical Library (Азбука веры - Богослужебные указания)**
  - **URL:** [https://azbyka.ru/bogosluzhenie/](https://azbyka.ru/bogosluzhenie/)
  - **Description:** Complete digital archive of Eastern Orthodox liturgical books (*Trebnik*, *Triodion*, *Pentecostarion*, *Menaion*, *Typikon*, *Oktoechos*) in Church Slavonic and Russian.
- **Orthlib / Pravoslavie.ru (Православная библиотека)**
  - **URL:** [http://orthlib.ru/](http://orthlib.ru/)
  - **Description:** Digitized Slavonic *Trebnik* (Book of Needs) and *Typikon* (Order of Divine Service).

### 2. Byzantine Greek & English (Βυζαντινή Γλώσσα & Αγγλικά)
- **AGES Initiatives - Digital Chant Stand (Ecumenical Patriarchate / GOARCH)**
  - **URL:** [https://www.agesinitiatives.com/dcs/public/dcs/dcs.html](https://www.agesinitiatives.com/dcs/public/dcs/dcs.html)
  - **Description:** Comprehensive bilingual (Greek/English) liturgical texts for the Divine Liturgies, Vespers, Matins, and Sacraments.
- **Apostoliki Diakonia of the Church of Greece (Αποστολική Διακονία)**
  - **URL:** [http://www.apostoliki-diakonia.gr/](http://www.apostoliki-diakonia.gr/)
  - **Description:** Official liturgical texts issued by the Church of Greece.

### 3. German Authorized Translations (Deutsche Übersetzungen)
- **Orthodoxe Bischofskonferenz in Deutschland (OBKD)**
  - **URL:** [https://www.obkd.de/](https://www.obkd.de/)
  - **Description:** Officially approved German translations of the Divine Liturgy of St. John Chrysostom and core sacraments.
- **Orthodoxe Gemeindebibliothek Berlin (Orthodoxia.de)**
  - **URL:** [http://www.orthodoxia.de/](http://www.orthodoxia.de/)
  - **Description:** Bilingual German/Slavonic liturgical publications and parallel texts.
- **Kloster Buchhagen / Anastasis-Verlag**
  - **Description:** High-level German Orthodox liturgical translations of the Oktoechos, Horologion, and Trebnik.

## Canonical Liturgical Books & Structure Definitions

1. **Horologion (Часослов / ⌚ Stundenbuch)**: Fixed daily prayers (Hours, Vespers, Matins).
2. **Euchologion / Trebnik (Евхологион / Требник / 🕊️ Buch der Sakramente)**: Baptism, Chrismation, Matrimony/Crowning, Unction, Funerals.
3. **Lenten Triodion (Триодь Постная / 💜 Fasten-Triodion)**: Great Lent & Holy Week services (Presanctified Liturgy, Great Canon).
4. **Pentecostarion / Festal Triodion (Триодь Цветная / 🕯️ Oster-Triodion)**: Pascha (Easter) to Pentecost.
5. **Menaion (Минея - 12 Bände / 📅 Monatsbände)**: Fixed feasts (Christmas, Theophany, Annunciation) and saints' days.
6. **Typikon (Типикон / 📜 Regiebuch)**: The master rubric book orchestrating all service combinations.
