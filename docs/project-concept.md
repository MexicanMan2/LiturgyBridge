# LiturgyBridge Project Concept

## Overview

LiturgyBridge is an open-source digital platform designed to support Orthodox communities by connecting liturgical tradition, multilingual accessibility, and community communication.

The project combines three main areas:

1. Liturgical Companion
2. Community Platform
3. Knowledge and Tradition Preservation

The goal is to build a sustainable digital foundation that helps people participate, understand, and connect.

---

# 1. Liturgical Companion

## Purpose

Many Orthodox services are celebrated in traditional languages such as:

- Church Slavonic
- Greek
- Arabic
- Romanian
- Georgian
- other liturgical languages

These languages are an essential part of the tradition, but they can create barriers for visitors, younger generations, and international communities.

LiturgyBridge helps people follow services in their own language while respecting the original liturgical tradition.
